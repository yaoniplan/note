- #### Use "xclip"
    - ``echo "`date +%F_%T`.gif" | xclip -selection clipboard``
- ***Notes***
    - `xclip -selection clipboard` # The same as `xsel --input --clipboard`
    - `mpv --speed=2 --fullscreen --mute=yes $(xsel --output --clipboard)` # Paste by default

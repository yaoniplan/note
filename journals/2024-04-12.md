- #### Use "date"
    - `date +%s` # Seconds since the Epoch (1970-01-01 00:00 UTC)
        - (e.g. 1712921641)
- ***Notes***
    - `+%s%3N` # In milliseconds
        - `3` # The first three digits
        - `N` # Nanoseconds
    - Because to generate UNIX timestamp.
- ***References***
    - `man date` # `/%s`

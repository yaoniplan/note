- #### Disable cache when develop a website
    - Chromium (Or Firefox)
        - Open developer tools (`Ctrl-Shift-i`)
        - Click on the *Network* tab (`Ctrl-]`)
        - Check the *Disable cache* checkbox
        - Refresh page (`Ctrl-r`)
